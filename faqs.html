FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What payment methods are supported?

A: We accept Cash on Delivery and Online Payment (e.g., Cash, Bank) on all of our products.

Q: Do you accept cash on delivery (COD)?

A: Yes, we accept Cash on Delivery (COD) on all of our products.

Q: Can I customize the size of the bracelets I will order?

A: Yes, you can customize the size of the bracelets. Kindly check out with the option 'custom' and add the preferred size to the notes before checking out.

Q: When are you shipping my order?

A: We usually ship orders 1-3 days after you have placed your order. This may depend on the availability of the product and the courier.

Q: How long will the shipping take?

A: The duration of shipping may depend on your location and the courier. For reference, we are located in Laguna.

Q: I have received a defective/damaged product, what can I do?

A: In case of a defective/damaged product, please get in touch with us by clicking 'Chat with Seller'. Kindly send us your concern and a picture/video proof of the damage/defect.

Q: I have received less or wrong product/size, what can I do?

A: In case of a missing/wrong product, we highly advise our customers to take a video before opening the parcel. Kindly send us the video of the unboxing by clicking 'Chat with Seller'. In the case where no video was taken, please do not hesitate to contact us to resolve the matter.
